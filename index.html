About us

We build apps people actually use

Gleria Studio is an indie project built by a single developer tired of overcomplicated apps. Simple ideas, well executed, free for everyone.

01 · Origin

Born from real frustrations

Every app in our portfolio started as a personal problem. Nestly when I needed a budget tracker without subscriptions. Space Focus when I could not find a simple focus timer that did not feel like a productivity cult. We do not build to fill a market gap. We build because we need it ourselves.

02 · How we build

Native Android, modern stack

React Native and Expo give us native speed without sacrificing development agility. TypeScript keeps the code reliable. Zustand for state, Supabase when a backend is needed, AdMob for monetization that stays out of the way. Built to last.

03 · What we stand for

Free. Private.

All apps are free to download. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no accounts required. Most features work fully offline and your data stays on your device. We earn through non-intrusive ads, never by selling your information.

Purpose

Every feature exists for a reason. We cut what does not add real value.

Performance

Smooth, fast, and stable on any Android device. Speed is not optional.

Design

UI/UX crafted to the pixel. Interfaces that feel natural from the first tap.

Privacy

Your data belongs to you. Offline-first, no tracking, no accounts.
